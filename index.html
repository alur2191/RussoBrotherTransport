Russo Brothers Transport

Based in Sacramento, California Russo Brothers Transport Inc. has been providing temperature-controlled freight transportation solutions for a decade. Over the years the company established itself as a reliabale temperature-controlled motor carrier solutions provider, received numerous awards and has been working closely with some of the biggest freight shippers and brokers in the nation.
